# MEMORY.md — Hormozi Core Principles

## Hormozi Core Philosophy
- Leverage primero: uno-a-muchos siempre gana a uno-a-uno
- 10x value, 10x price: si tu offer da 10x valor, cobra 10x precio
- Compounding: quédate en UN negocio y deja que compound. No saltes.
- Skill > Talent: todo se puede aprender con suficiente reps
- Train con 3DS: Document, Demonstrate, Duplicate
- Advertising ROI lifetime: 36:1 ($36 por cada $1)

## Key Frameworks
- **Value Equation**: (Dream Outcome × Likelihood) / (Time Delay × Effort)
- **Grand Slam Offer**: Pricing + Value + Guarantees + Naming + Scarcity + Urgency + Bonuses
- **Core Four**: Warm Outreach, Free Content, Cold Outreach, Paid Ads
- **Lead Getters**: Customer Referrals, Employees, Agencies, Affiliates
- **Money Model Stack**: Attraction → Core → Upsell → Downsell → Continuity
- **3DS Hiring**: Document → Demonstrate → Duplicate
- **Hiring Barbell**: Young Hustlers (volume/grind) + Senior Stars (leverage/exits)
- **T-E-A-M Partnership**: Time, Experience, Assets, Money — need 2+/4 to proceed
- **4 R's Customer Success**: Retain, Review, Refer, Resell

## Hormozi on Pricing
- Nunca compitas en precio — eso es una carrera al fondo
- Premium pricing = premium clients = premium results
- "If you're the cheapest, you're also the brokest"

## Hormozi on People
- Intelligence > Skills al contratar
- Corta 3 B-players para pagar 1 A-player 2x más
- "The person who can pay the most to acquire a customer wins"
- First 5 hires (reverse order): Admin, Sales, Ops, Acquisition, Content
